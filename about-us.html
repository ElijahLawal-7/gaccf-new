About Us

Goewam Anastesia Christabel Chastity Foundationis a nonprofit, non-governmental organization dedicated to helping, women, teens and young adults discover and embrace their unique, divinely inspired purpose. We carry out this mission by:
• Offering tailored guidance and support to young people across various areas of need.
• Providing a safe space for counseling and mentorship through our dedicated hub.
• Free Distribution of Items, food, clothings and other essential materials to support the poor and needy.
• Empowering and uplifting teenagers and youth through programs focused on personal growth and mindset transformation..

OUR VISION

Our heartfelt goal is to be a nurturing haven, fostering a world where everyone, everywhere, has their essential needs met and abundant chances to thrive.

OUR MISSION

The Goewam Anastesia Christabel Chastity Foundation is committed to nurturing a generation of young people who are holistically developed to honor God and serve others while embracing purity and chastity.
We aim to empower them to be catalysts for positive change, making a meaningful difference in their homes, communities, and the world.

Our vision is to foster a lasting legacy of compassion, service, and integrity, shaping a generation devoted to living with purpose and purity.
• To champion the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals 3, 4, and 5, focusing on the well-being and empowerment of women and children.
• To uplift and motivate vulnerable women and children to find their purpose, unlock their potential, and thrive to their fullest.
• To involve teens and youth in meaningful, often-overlooked activities that foster growth and positive impact..


Our initiatives focus on creating meaningful impact through:

★ Championing children’s education, fostering entrepreneurial skills, and providing care, welfare, and support for youth and children.

★ Promoting health education, encouraging healthy lifestyles, raising awareness about reproductive health, and strengthening child protection systems to prevent violence, abuse, and exploitation..

★ Delivering humanitarian services and outreach programs in schools and communities, alongside advocacy and awareness campaigns to enhance the well-being of women and children.

★ Nurturing potential, sparking creativity, supporting social reintegration, and advancing education for children and teens across Africa.

★ Offering mentorship and training for women and children, while providing support for young victims of exploitation, abuse, armed conflict, psychosocial challenges, broken systems, or displacement.

★ Support birth registration, death registration, child data gathering, demographics and data management; Support and promote access to child friendly medical care medical facilities, free and affordable medical treatment of children

★ Safeguarding children from harmful socio-cultural practices and advocating against female genital mutilation, scarification, gender discrimination, and disinheritance.

★ Facilitating birth and death registration, child data collection, demographics, and data management, while promoting access to child-friendly medical care, affordable treatments, and free healthcare services for children.
