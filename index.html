Our Values

Innovation

“We don’t just respond—we reimagine.”
At GACCF, innovation drives our mission. We apply fresh thinking to tackle deep-rooted challenges—from digital inclusion to sustainable health solutions. Whether through community-driven ideas or tech-enabled programs, we empower people to be part of the solution. We believe: Hope multiplies when creativity meets compassion.

Transparency

“Trust is earned with every act of clarity.”
We are deeply committed to openness in how we operate, spend, and serve. Our donors, partners, and beneficiaries are kept informed through detailed reporting, transparent audits, and shared decision-making. We uphold: Integrity in action, accountability in every step.

Accessibility & Inclusivity

“No one is left behind—because everyone matters.”
Our programs are designed to reach the unreached. From rural schools and refugee camps to marginalized urban neighborhoods, we ensure every voice is heard and every need is met.

Our Vision

A society where every child and youth is seen as an integral and is given a level playing field to attain their full potential required to transform our world!

Our Mission

• To promote the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals 3,4,&5 indicators related to women and children.
• To inspire vulnerable women and children to discover purpose, and unleash and maximise their potential.
• To engage youth and teenagers in productive activities that have been subtly neglected.

Our Community

Our community comprises of passionate volunteers, social impact workers, and skilled professionals actively advancing the UNSDGs and striving relentlessly to improve the world, particularly for girls and children.

Our Outreaches

GACCF runs several programmes, including: Medical and skill development outreaches
- School and orphanage outreaches
- Mentorship and leadership training
- Special events and workshops
- Health education - Advocacy and awareness campaigns.
These programmes empower individuals and communities through education, skill-building, and support.

Notes From The Founder

We started out in 2018, I had just finished my WAEC and JAMB but the passion has been there to render help to vulnerable youths especially those going through sexual molestation and abuse, mental inhealth and all that.

I began from my neighborhood and then in 2020 it turned out to be a season of intensed prayer retreat for myself and six other amazing sisters. It broke out and we had something like a mini Bible study and prayer session in my house every week.
Of course, I gained admission into the university in 2018 as well and resumed fully in 2019 January. In 2022 we moved out of the rented apartment and so we all scattered, different places, schools and all. It was formerly GAC SEXUAL PURITY when we had not registered and upon several attempts to get same name registered with the CAC it was not approved hence the slight change in name

In 2023, God spoke on the need to get serious and that was the beginning of intentionality because before now, it has always been a status thing, or Facebook thing and basically personal counseling thing. We had impacted over 1000+ souls online at that time.

We launched it officially in September first week, 2023 with a record of over 200 Global audience and then in 2024, we grew to over 1000+ audience in the WhatsApp groups. The TalkShows is usually every first Saturday of the month. This has been in place since September, 2023 till date.

The journey of the legal registration began when we got the vision of hosting a conference for teens in schools per state and in other countries. We became a legally registered entity in March, 2025.
Presently, we have solid structures on ground, incredible volunteers and an enthusiastic team who have caught this audacious vision. This is our journey of becoming.
