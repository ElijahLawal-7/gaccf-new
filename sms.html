Single Mother Syndrome

Single Mother Syndrome

Mistakes happen, yet not expected to be a death sentence, our obvious beliefs as Excellent Achievers Network International. Every girl child counted victims, deserves another opportunity, and the non-victimised deserves to be shielded from such a trap.

This intervention focuses on the girl child with the aim of instigating self-awareness for our prospects in the slums. We are committed to ensuring that the girl child finds purpose and sees value in what lies within her. This intervention aligns with the Agenda 2063 of the African Union and the 5th goal of the sustainable development goals of the United Nations.
This goal promotes gender equality with a drive to empower all women and girls. It is our common belief that gender equality is both a girl's fundamental right and premise for a fulfilling, and sustainable life.

This intervention is what birthed the annual events called LADIES TIME OUT. A girl based meeting that engages ladies of the "community low-class category" on a life transforming contact with successful women from all works of life who also rose from the slum. Quite interesting, we have been able to host four LATO programs since inception, from VIRTUOUS; FAR ABOVE RUBIES; THE GIRL CHILD, AND TECHNOLOGY to THE LADY OF WORTH.
These have changed the lives of many young ladies and increased the hope of believing in themselves, making a worth out of life without becoming a waste. October annually is the deal for this convocation. We look forward to partnering with you so we can engage more of these "girl child" who are not advantaged.
